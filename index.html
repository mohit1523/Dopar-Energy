About Us

Dopar Energy is an electric powertrain technology company specialize in developing Motor controllers for EV applications.
We develop customized BLDC Motor controllers as per the vehicle manufacturer's requirement which has cutting-edge features.
Our technology is capable of driving any radial flux as well as Axial flux motor topology.
The controller architecture is designed to be easily compatible with any hub, or mid-drive motor.

Vision

Become a leading e-powertrain company, which leads technology with sustainability.
