ABOUT

Dopar Energy is a startup company founded in year 2021, developing motor control technology for the Electric vehicle application. Our vision and core value is to provide accessible technology for tomorrow and to reduce the dependability on foreign technology. We provide quality and reliable products by means of localization. Our primary focus is on designing, developing and delivering high performance scalable products for the Electric Vehicle segment. We aim to provide quality and reliable products by means of made in India.
